The Agreement

This agreement is between the student (STUDENT) and Bootcamp Education, Inc. (DEV BOOTCAMP) regarding Ruby on Rails training program.

Training Course

DEV BOOTCAMP is committed to providing hands-on training in their San Francisco premises to STUDENT Monday through Friday from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. during the training period. Training will be a combination of lectures, curated content (books, screencasts, and tutorials), exercises, challenges, games, code competitions and reviews. Training will be instructor led, and student driven. There is no requirement for after-hours work, but it is expected that because of the intense nature of the training STUDENT might need to work evenings and weekends. Training will include some physical exercises and challenges that are optional. STUDENT assumes full responsibility for their physical and emotional wellbeing.

Tuition

STUDENT understands that deposit ($1000) is due within 7 days of signing this agreement. The remainder of full tuition is due 61 days before the start of their assigned cohort. The $1000 deposit is non-refundable.

Job Placement

DEV BOOTCAMP will make earnest efforts to connect STUDENT with tech companies and recruitment firms looking for job candidates. If STUDENT manages to successfully land a job through a connection that DEV BOOTCAMP makes, DEV BOOTCAMP will receive a contingency fee from the employer and refund student $3000 of their tuition and deposit. To be clear: the contingency fee does NOT come out of the student’s salary. It works similar to a recruiter fee. STUDENT is free to work for whomever they want and apply everywhere they are able.

STUDENT understands that there is no guarantee of job, or job interview, and that the partial refund of their tuition is based on STUDENT being offered a job, successfully placed, and staying in employment for 180 days in one of the companies DEV BOOTCAMP introduces them to.

Termination of this Agreement

STUDENT may leave the training during their first week and get all of their tuition back minus their $1000 deposit. DEV BOOTCAMP reserves the right to terminate STUDENT’s training at any point and return their prorated tuition to them.
