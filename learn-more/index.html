What will you learn?

  • The Ruby on Rails Application Framework

    Learn one of the best ways to build modern, database-backed web applications

  • HTML5 & CSS

    Master the fundamentals of both semantic markup as well as how to make your web applications render beautifully on any device.

  • JavaScript Fundamentals

    Grok techniques to add more life and interactivity to your applications on the client-side.

  • Test Driven Development

    Learn a methodology of producing rock-solid, easy-to-maintain code.

  • Agile Development

    Develop and iterate on your projects by keeping a close eye on costs and value.

  • Git and Source Control

    Manage your code versions, and collaborate on projects with other programmers.

  • Advanced Web Technologies

    jQuery, Coffeescript, SASS, HAML, AJAX, and other strange sounding four letter acronyms that will blow your mind.

  • Soft Skills

    Get your feet wet with pair-programming, presentation skills, interview skills, code reviews, giving feedback, and more.

The Dev Bootcamp Way

  • Hands on

    Learning

    Dev Bootcamp is not a typical classroom. Think of it more as apprenticeship or a workshop intensive. You will be learning by doing and building, not just by sitting back and listening. We set up bootcamp to closely resemble a real work environment.

  • Flipped

    Classroom

    Most classes give instruction to the group, then have people practice on their own. At Bootcamp, it's the other way around: you get instruction through a curated set of videos, books and tutorials. Practice happens by applying the theory in group projects.

  • Intensive

    Learning

    We pack a LOT of material into a very short timeframe and set the bar very high for you. Come prepared to work harder than you've ever worked in your life. You will go to sleep dreaming of code. Fun? Yes. Piece of cake? Nope.

  • Engaged

    Mentorship

    A big part of learning to be a developer is learning how to get unstuck. You don't have to do it alone. You will have plenty of one-on-one attention from mentors who can help you navigate the hard parts.
